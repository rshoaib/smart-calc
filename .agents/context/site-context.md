# DailySmartCalc — Site Context

> **URL**: https://dailysmartcalc.com
> **Stack**: Vite-React SPA + Vercel
> **Revenue**: AdSense
> **Core Value**: Free online calculators for finance and health — instant results, no signup, 100% private.

---

## 🎤 Brand Voice

- **Tone**: Helpful, clear, no-jargon. "Calculate your mortgage in seconds — free."
- **Style**: Short sentences. Tables for comparisons. Bold key numbers and results.
- **Address**: Second person ("you"). Empathetic to financial stress, never preachy.
- **Differentiator**: Always mention "free, instant, no signup" within the first 100 words.
- **Formatting**: Lead with the calculator CTA, then explain the topic below.
